Game idea:
You manage Fernhollow Bakery, a tiny enchanted pastry shop nestled inside the hollow base of a giant ancient oak tree in a sun-dappled forest. Forest creatures — hedgehogs, rabbits, foxes, and drowsy owls — visit throughout the day to order freshly baked pastries, warm drinks, and seasonal treats. You bake in a cluttered but cozy kitchen alcove, manage a small garden patch out back for fresh ingredients, serve customers across a wooden counter, and slowly decorate and expand the shop. The day ends when the lanterns dim, and your coins let you unlock new recipes, upgrade the kitchen, and plant new garden beds. The game loop is bake → serve → restock → upgrade, with a warm low-pressure rhythm.
